Device Grading Guide

ESN: Bad ESN is Accepted but devices cannot be stolen > utilizing checkmend.com

HSO: Within 30 days into warranty, No Model "N" Number number. ***ACTIVATED APPLE SWAP = GRADE A PRICE***

SWAP: Genuine Apple Swaps Only! "N" Model Number / NEVER ACTIVATED / ***ACTIVATED APPLE SWAP = GRADE A PRICE***

Asurion / Valutech: Phones that have been "Remaufactured" / "Refurbished" and are marked on the back > Can take $50 lower then "Grade B" pricing

NEW / Sealed: Brand NEW / Sealed in Box > Warranty not activated, check HERE

NEW / Open: Brand NEW / Open Box > Film on phone, all accessories sealed, warranty within 30 days of activation, check HERE

Sealed (Activated): Brand NEW / Sealed in Box > Warranty HAS BEEN activated, check HERE

Warranty: Newest models need to be covered under apple 1 year warranty. If not "Replaced" / Red imei, expired, etc. May be eligible for additional deduction

Grade A: Like New / Mint condition

Grade B: OEM, no cracks, Light Use, fully functional

Grade C: Cracked Glass / Good LCD, 1 x SMALL White Spot; Multiple / Major Spots = Bad LCD, Must be OEM

Grade D: Bad LCD, Touch Issues, Must be fully functional phone just needs new screen. After Market also = Bad LCD ***BLACK LCD / UNABLE TO TEST = DOA***

DOA: No Power / Signs of Life; Cracked, Bad LCD, Water damaged, mixed issues TOO HEAVY BEND / DAMAGE / MISSING PARTS MAY BE SUBJECT TO LOWER PRICE

"Service" / Degraded Battery %: Batteries that show "Service" / are degraded (generally under 80% battery) - Subject to deduction

"Genuine / Used" Repair Messgaes: "Genuine" / "Used" Repair Messages are subject to deduction. (ie. Display / Battery / Camera, etc.) / Will do our best but varies depending on device

"Unknown" Repair Messgaes: Feel free to ASK, but items will be deducted

LCD Burns: **COMMON ISSUE TO LOOK OUT FOR** - Images (keyboard / apps burnt into LCD > check for on white screen) Mostly seen on Samsung / iPhone X and newer

Bad Face ID: **COMMON ISSUE TO LOOK OUT FOR** - Face ID MUST be fully working, test Face ID and make sure it reads / registers your face (not just shows it ; but not read)

!!! Camera Spotting !!!: **COMMON ISSUE TO LOOK OUT FOR** - Commony issue for i12 / i13 series (other series can have too) ~ Check Zoomed in (2.5x - 3x zoom) - Subject to additional deduction

***HELPFUL HINT***: Check phones for True Tone > Missing True Tone = High Likelihood of Aftermarket Screen (Not 100% of the time, but most of the time screen will be aftermarket)

HEAVY SCRATCHING: Subject to additional deduction

HEAVY EDGE WEAR: Subject to additional deduction

Aftermarket (AM) Screen: Aftermaket Screen = Bad LCD

Small (1 x) LCD Spot: Used = Cracked price; If Cracked = OK

Cracked Lens: Subject to additional deduction / Check camera (including different zooms) often Cracked Lens can have heavy spotting also - Subject to additional deduction

Unlocked Relocks / KG Lock: **NO TIME LIMIT** ~ if phone were to relock after sale, or Samsung becomes Trade In / KG Lock down the line seller is responsible for Deduction or can Return
