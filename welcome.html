Device Grading Guide

ESN: Electronic Serial Number — device identification.

HSO: Handset Sold Outright — device paid in full, eligible for unlocking.

SWAP: Swapped device in exchange program.

NEW Sealed: Original sealed packaging, never opened.

NEW Open Box: Opened but never used; may have minor packaging wear.

Sealed (Activated): Sealed original packaging but device has been activated.

Grade A: Excellent — minimal visible wear, fully functional. Small scratches acceptable.

Grade B: Good — moderate wear visible, fully functional. Some scratches/minor dents.

Grade C: Fair — heavy wear visible, fully functional. Noticeable scratches/dents, possible minor LCD burn.

Grade D: Poor — significant wear, fully functional. Heavy scratches, dents, possible cosmetic defects.

DOA: Dead On Arrival — device does not power on or has critical hardware failure.

Warranty: All devices sold as-is unless otherwise negotiated.

Service Battery: Battery has been replaced by Apple or certified repair center.

Genuine/Used Repair: Device has been repaired using genuine parts or aftermarket parts respectively.

LCD Burns: Image persistence or burn-in visible on screen.

Bad Face ID: Face recognition feature non-functional.

Camera Spotting: Dust or debris visible inside camera lens.

True Tone: Display color temperature calibration feature.

Heavy Scratching: Multiple deep scratches visible across device surface.

Heavy Edge Wear: Significant wear on device edges.

Aftermarket Screen: Non-original display replacement.

Small LCD Spot: Minor discoloration or dead pixel.

Cracked Lens: Camera lens glass damaged.

Unlocked Relocks/KG Lock: Device may relock if not properly managed.
